Privacy Policy for Audiline

Last updated: March 08, 2026

Audiline is a mobile app, it provides short audio news summaries. We collect minimal data, and only what you choose to provide.

Read/listened history

To improve your experience and avoid repeating the same news items, the app stores a local record of what you've listened to on your device only. This data never leaves your device, is not sent to our servers, and is not shared with anyone. You can clear this history in the app settings if available.

We do not collect any other personal data

This includes:

  • ● No names, email, phone numbers, or other contact info
  • ● No location data.
  • ● No device IDs, advertising IDs, or analytics sent off-device.
  • ● No usage tracking beyond local history.
  • ● No cookies or tracking technologies.

App Permissions

The app requests permission to play audio in the background or on the lock screen. This is solely for media playback and does not collect or transmit any personal data.

Third-Party Content

All news content comes from public third-party sources. We do not host or control it, and any links to originals are for convenience—we are not responsible for their privacy practices.

Data Security

Any optional email data we receive is handled securely. Local history is stored only on your device using standard Android storage.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with a new date. Continued use of the app means you accept the updates.
