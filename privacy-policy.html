Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Audiline ("we," "us," or "our") provides a mobile platform for curated audio news summaries. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data and create our content.

1

Editorial Transparency & Content Creation

Audiline is a human led short audio news platform. Our mission is to provide significant transformative value by condensing complex information into a concise, audio-native format.

  • Original Summarization — Every news summary in Audiline is an original work written and edited by our team. We synthesize information from public news events to create unique "Executive Summaries" designed for hands-free consumption.
  • Manual Verification — To ensure accuracy and quality, every summary is researched, written, and verified by our editorial team before being published in the app.
  • Source Attribution — We respect the work of the broader journalistic community. While our summaries are original, we provide attribution to the primary news sources for the underlying facts reported.
2

Account Deletion & Data Rights

You have full control over your data.

  • Deletion Request — If you wish to delete your account or any personal data (Name, Email, or Phone), please email us at audiline.news@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request."
  • Process — Upon receiving your request, we will permanently delete your personal information and account from our servers within 30 days.
3

Local Data (On-Device Only)

  • Listening History — To help you keep track of what you've heard, the app stores your history locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers and can be cleared at any time in the app settings.
4

Permissions

  • Media Playback — We request permission to play audio in the background and on the lock screen. This is strictly for the app's media player functionality.

Questions about this policy?

For any questions regarding this policy or to exercise your data rights, reach out anytime.
